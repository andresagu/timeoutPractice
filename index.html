Game Rules

Welcome!

You'll be given a random target number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking the you will add a specific amount to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random target number, you lose if your number goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the target and crystal values will change.
